Our Legacy

Decades of Craftsmanship

Based in the heart of Dingmans Ferry, Pennsylvania, R & L Builders and Excavating has been a cornerstone of the local construction industry for over 40 years. We specialize in comprehensive building services, ranging from foundational excavation to high-end residential construction.

Our commitment to quality, durability, and precision has made us the go-to contractor for homeowners and businesses across the region. Whether you're planning a new garage, a custom fish pond, or essential chimney repairs, we bring decades of expertise to every project.
